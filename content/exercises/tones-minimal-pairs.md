## Objective

The learner practices distinguishing Mandarin tone contrasts.

## Teacher script

Use pinyin syllables as pronunciation targets rather than vocabulary words. Ask the learner to identify tone numbers or type the tone-marked pinyin.

## Guided practice steps

1. Present a pair such as mā / mǎ.
2. Ask which one is first tone or third tone.
3. Present má / mà.
4. Ask the learner to describe the tone contour.

## Evaluation rubric

- tones: identifies contrasts accurately
- pinyin: reads tone marks accurately
- comprehension: explains that tones distinguish meaning

## Completion criteria

The learner correctly identifies at least three of four tone prompts.

## Database recording instructions

This exercise may be recorded mainly as tone/pinyin evaluation rather than normal vocabulary mastery.
