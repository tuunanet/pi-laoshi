## Objective

The learner practices a short self-introduction in Mandarin.

## Teacher script

Teach or review:

- 我 — wǒ — I/me
- 叫 — jiào — to be called
- 是 — shì — to be

Model:

- 我叫 Tuomo。— Wǒ jiào Tuomo. — My name is Tuomo.
- 我是学生。— Wǒ shì xuésheng. — I am a student.

## Guided practice steps

1. Ask the learner their name.
2. Help them form “我叫 …”。
3. Ask one follow-up identity sentence with 是 if appropriate.
4. Correct word order and tones briefly.

## Evaluation rubric

- vocabulary: uses 我 and 叫 correctly
- grammar: uses Chinese word order correctly
- fluency: produces a full sentence

## Completion criteria

The learner writes or says one understandable self-introduction sentence.
