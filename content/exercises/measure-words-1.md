## Objective

The learner gets initial practice with the common measure word 个.

## Teacher script

Explain that Mandarin often uses a measure word between a number and noun. 个 (gè) is a very common general measure word.

Examples:

- 一个人 — yí gè rén — one person
- 三个学生 — sān gè xuésheng — three students

## Guided practice steps

1. Ask the learner to translate “one person.”
2. Ask the learner to translate “three students.”
3. Correct missing 个 gently.
4. Ask one learner-generated sentence if they are ready.

## Evaluation rubric

- vocabulary: recognizes 个
- grammar: places 个 between number and noun
- fluency: produces a short noun phrase

## Completion criteria

The learner produces at least one correct number + 个 + noun phrase.
