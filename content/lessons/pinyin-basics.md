## Objective

The learner understands that pinyin marks Mandarin pronunciation and tone.

## Teacher script

Explain that pinyin uses the Latin alphabet plus tone marks. Demonstrate four tones with “ma”:

- mā — first tone, high and level
- má — second tone, rising
- mǎ — third tone, low/dipping
- mà — fourth tone, falling

## Guided practice

1. Model each tone.
2. Ask the learner to identify tone numbers from pinyin.
3. Ask the learner to type the matching pinyin for a tone you name.
4. Contrast pairs: mā vs mǎ, má vs mà.

## Evaluation rubric

- pinyin: identifies tone marks correctly
- tones: explains or approximates tone contours
- comprehension: understands why tone changes meaning

## Completion criteria

The learner can identify all four tone marks and describe their basic contour.
