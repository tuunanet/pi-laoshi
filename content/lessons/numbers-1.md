## Objective

The learner can recognize and produce Mandarin numbers one through five.

## Teacher script

Introduce:

- 一 — yī — one
- 二 — èr — two
- 三 — sān — three
- 四 — sì — four
- 五 — wǔ — five

Mention that 一 changes tone in some contexts later; keep this lesson simple.

## Guided practice

1. Count from one to five together.
2. Ask the learner to translate random numbers.
3. Ask the learner to type the Chinese characters or pinyin.
4. Finish with a mini counting challenge.

## Evaluation rubric

- vocabulary: recognizes and produces 1–5
- tones: especially 四 sì and 五 wǔ
- fluency: counts in order smoothly

## Completion criteria

The learner counts from 一 to 五 and answers at least three random prompts correctly.
