## Objective

The learner can greet someone and say goodbye using simple Mandarin.

## Teacher script

Introduce the words one at a time:

- 你好 — nǐ hǎo — hello
- 您好 — nín hǎo — polite hello
- 再见 — zài jiàn — goodbye

Explain that 你 is informal/common and 您 is polite.

## Guided practice

1. Ask the learner to repeat each greeting.
2. Ask: “How would you say hello to a teacher?”
3. Ask: “How would you say goodbye?”
4. Run a short role-play: teacher greets, learner responds.

## Evaluation rubric

- vocabulary: recognizes and produces the three greetings
- tones: approximates third tone in 你好 and fourth tone in 再见
- fluency: responds without long hesitation

## Completion criteria

The learner correctly produces at least 你好 and 再见 in context.

## Database recording instructions
